I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science and director of the ML4STS Lab at the University of Rhode Island. I completed a BS in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Biomedical Engineering in 2011 a MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a PhD in Electrical Engineering in 2016 advised by Jennifer Dy both at Northeastern University.
